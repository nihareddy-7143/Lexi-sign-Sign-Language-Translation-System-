// THREE PILLARS

One platform. Infinite conversations.

LexiSign is built around three tightly integrated modules — each solving a critical piece of the sign-language accessibility puzzle.

Module 01

Sign → Text & Voice

A custom-trained ML model recognizes sign language gestures in real time. NLP stitches them into meaningful sentences, then translates to any of 10 Indian languages and speaks them aloud.

Real-time gesture recognition
10+ Indian language output
Natural text-to-speech voice
Module 02

Text / Voice → Avatar Signs

Type or speak in English or any Indian language. A 3D avatar instantly performs the corresponding sign language — closing the communication loop both ways.

Multilingual text & voice input
Realistic 3D avatar rendering
Cross-language sign synthesis
Module 03

Gamified Learning

Learn alphabets, numbers, words and phrases through level-based challenges. An adaptive engine personalizes difficulty so every learner progresses at their own pace.

Level-based skill progression
Adaptive difficulty engine
Live gesture validation
